##  About The Foundation
The **AMA-TUALU FOUNDATION** is dedicated to unlocking the limitless potential of youth across Africa. Registered in Uganda and operating from the West Nile Region (Adjumani), we serve youth and young professionals aged 15–35 who seek personal development, financial independence, and community transformation through ethical, revelation-driven strategies.

##  Our Five Pillars
1. **Youth Leadership**: Platforms to find a voice, build confidence, and take on active roles.
2. **Digital Literacy**: Access to computers, coding bootcamps, and modern software training.
3. **Mentorship**: One-on-one executive coaching, career counseling, and academic support.
4. **Community Impact**: From environmental clean-ups to financial literacy workshops.
5. **Membership & Community**: "The Wisdom Box" curated monthly program.

##  Website Features

## ⛪ About The Parish
St. Mary's Lake View Catholic Parish is situated in Lwampanga, Nakasongola District, serving the spiritual and communal needs of the local fishing and farming community. Our mission is to foster a vibrant community of faith through the sacraments, pastoral care, and fellowship.

## 🌟 Our Core Pillars & Ministries
1. **Liturgy & Worship**: Weekly Mass schedules, adoration, and sacramental life.
2. **Community Fellowship**: Parish groups, youth catechesis, and family support.
3. **Pastoral Care & Outreach**: Support for the sick, elderly, and vulnerable in the Nakasongola community.
4. **Youth & Catechesis**: Nurturing the next generation of faithful through religious instruction and fellowship.
5. **Parish Membership**: Spiritual resources, strategies for ethical community growth, and parishioner engagement.

## 🌐 Website Features
- **Modern & Premium UI/UX:** Built with high-end visual design principles, dynamic animations, and warm colors (Navy, Terracotta, Gold).
- **Fully Responsive Layout:** Optimized for Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop viewing.
- **Scroll Reveal Animations:** Micro-interactions that bring the page to life as users scroll.
- **Interactive Donation & Offertory Form:** Seamless user flow for selecting and inputting support for parish ministries.

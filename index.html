Core Identity

A community built on faith, hope, and love in action.

St Mary's Lake View Catholic Parish, Lwampanga, is dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth and serving the pastoral and sacramental needs of our community. We believe that through prayer, active faith, and community development, we can walk together in the footsteps of Christ and make a lasting difference in Nakasongola District.

Serving Lwampanga Town Council, Budiebo Constituency, Nakasongola District, and the surrounding areas, we offer a welcoming space for prayer, fellowship, and active participation in the Church's mission of charity, education, and development.

Our Mission

"To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, celebrate the holy sacraments, and serve our community through faith formation, spiritual mentorship, and charitable outreach."

  • Sacramental celebration, prayer groups, and liturgical worship
  • Faith education, Sunday school, and youth mentorship
  • Actively serving Nakasongola, Lwampanga, and outstations

The Pastoral Need

Lwampanga and its surrounding fishing communities on Lake Kyoga face unique social and spiritual challenges, including high poverty rates, limited educational access, and a need for strong faith formation.

Our Response

By establishing active outstations, nurturing youth through Sunday school, and providing family support, St. Mary's brings sacramental grace, active charity, and hope directly to the heart of Nakasongola.
